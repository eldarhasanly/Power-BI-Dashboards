# Data Jobs Dashboard v2 w/ Power BI

![Dashboard Overview](img/Dashboard%20Overview.png)

## Introduction

Navigating the data job market often feels overwhelming, with insights spread across countless sources. This **V2.0 dashboard** is built for **job seekers, career switchers, and professionals considering a move** to simplify that process. Leveraging a real-world dataset of **2024 data science job postings**, complete with information on roles, salaries, and locations, the project delivers a clean, single-page view that makes it easy to uncover key market trends and pay benchmarks at a glance.

## Methodologies Implemented

- **Dashboard Design:** Designing a clean, user-friendly, and visually engaging report layout.
- **Power Query ETL:** Cleaning, transforming, and shaping raw data for analysis.
- **Data Modeling:** Developing optimized data models with well-defined relationships, following Star Schema best practices.
- **DAX Fundamentals:** Building measures and calculations to generate meaningful insights and aggregations.
- **Visualizations Used:**
    - **Core Charts:** Column, bar, line, and area charts to analyze comparisons and trends.
    - **Map Visuals:** Visualizing geographic and location-based data.
    - **Cards:** Showcasing key metrics and KPIs at a glance.
    - **Tables:** Displaying detailed, structured data.
    - **Chart Diversity:** Leveraging both standard and less common chart types to enhance data storytelling.
- **Interactive Elements:**
    - **Slicers:** Allowing users to dynamically filter and explore the data.
    - **Buttons & Bookmarks:** Enabling smooth navigation and controlled report views, including drill-through functionality.

## Dashboard Overview

This second iteration brings the dashboard into a **single, streamlined page**, purpose-built to provide job seekers with immediate access to the most important market insights.

![Dashboard](img/Dashboard%20Overview.png)  

This page serves as a compact **mission control hub** for the data job market, presenting essential KPIs such as **Job Count, Skills per Job, Median Annual Salary, and Median Hourly Salary**. It also enables quick insights into **skill demand** (by percentage or total postings) and straightforward comparisons of **salary levels across job titles**, all optimized for a fast, high-level view of the market.

## Conclusion

This updated **Version 2.0 dashboard** demonstrates how Power BI can turn large volumes of job posting data into a focused, high-impact career analysis tool. It supports **job seekers, career switchers, and professionals exploring new roles** by enabling efficient filtering and exploration of critical market insights on a single page—helping users make confident, data-driven decisions about their next career step.
